WHAT I BUILD

Custom Systems

Each system is custom-designed using tools like Airtable (and others, based on your needs). Think of it as your all-in-one command center—built specifically for how you work.

Custom Database Design

I’ll build a structured, easy-to-use system to centralize your business data—so everything you need is in one place. Here are a few examples of what your custom database can manage:

  • • Organize client details, service history, and notes
  • • Track jobs, appointments, and progress
  • • Store intake forms, contracts, and more

Workflow Automation

I’ll set up smart, behind-the-scenes workflows that save you time and minimize manual work. The examples below show just a few ways automation can streamline your daily operations:

  • • New client fills out a form → auto-added to your CRM
  • • Appointment booked → calendar event + reminder
  • • Project completed → invoice auto-generated

I’ll design and set up automated workflows that handle repetitive tasks for you—so you can focus on running your business, not managing logistics. For example, I can configure your system to automatically capture new client details from intake forms and store them in your CRM. I can also link your booking tool to your calendar, so that when an appointment is scheduled, it triggers an event creation and sends out confirmations or reminders. And when a project is marked complete, I can build in a workflow that auto-generates an invoice and either sends it out or flags it for your review.

Tool Integrations

I can connect the tools you already use so they work better together—with less effort on your part. The examples below show just a few ways I can streamline your tech stack:

  • • Connect tools like Calendly, QuickBooks, Gmail, Slack
  • • Automate across platforms with Zapier or Make
  • • Build bridges between website, CRM, and more
